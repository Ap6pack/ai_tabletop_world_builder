### Option 1: Annual Subscription
- Pay yearly for continuous access
- Includes updates and support
- Renewal discounts available
- Price locked for multi-year agreements

### Option 2: Perpetual License
- One-time payment for indefinite use
- Maintenance and support sold separately (typically 20% annual fee)

- Typically 10-20% depending on volume
- Aligned incentives for mutual success
- Detailed reporting and auditing included
